BROWNIES N WAYS

Kinds of Brownies

Brownies were the first kind of dessert I ever made. As an ok baker, I don't think I am qualified to give any advice, hence this website is only a compilation of my collection of recipes.

As a brownie enthusiast, I keep track of various recipes. There may be blogs, websites or videos boasting of "The Best Brownie Ever" or "The Most Chocolaty/ Fudgey/ Cakey Brownie From Scratch", but let's be honest, that's just clickbait. I've definitely been there, you've probably been there too!
