BROWNIES N WAYS

KETO BROWNIES

These brownies are the fudgy kind, with a healthier twist to it as it is low carb.

This recipe uses almond flour instead of regular flour, hence it is flourless and gluten free. For this recipe, dutch processed cocoa is recommended as the recipe mimics boxed brownie.

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 17 minutes
  • Total time: 37 minutes
  • Servings: 15-25 brownies
    •

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fine almond flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp dutch cocoa
  • Â½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil or butter
  • 3 tbsp water or additional oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup sugar of preference
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract

Method/ Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/ 350°F. Lightly grease a 9" x 13" pan and line with parchment.
  2. Combine the melted butter and sugar, stirring until smooth.
  3. Stir in the cocoa, salt, baking powder and vanilla.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Scrape the bowl, then add the water, stirring until smooth. Add in the flour and fold it in.
  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake the brownies for 20 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, or with a few moist crumbs attached. The brownies should feel set both on the edges and in the center.
  7. Remove the brownies from the oven, and cool them on a rack before cutting and serving. Glaze or frost, if desired.
